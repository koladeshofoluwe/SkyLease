Welcome to the SkyLease Rental application! Here you will learn the requirement to rent an aircraft and how to get approved to rent aircrafts!

Rental Requirement

  • Must hold a valid pilot's license (PPL or higher).
  • Minimum of 50 logged flight hours
  • Completed checkout flight with SkyLease instructor
  • Proof of renter's insurance or purchase of SkyLease coverage.
  • Must be at least 18 years of age.
