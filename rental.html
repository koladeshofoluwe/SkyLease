Welcome to the SkyLease Rental application! Here you will learn the requirement to rent an aircraft and how to get approved to rent aircrafts!

Rental Requirement

  • Must hold a valid pilot's license (PPL or higher).
  • Minimum of 50 logged flight hours
  • Completed checkout flight with SkyLease instructor
  • Proof of renter's insurance or purchase of SkyLease coverage.
  • Must be at least 18 years of age.

How to obtain a Skylease License

  • Fill out the information listed on the side
  • Upload your license and your flight hours, a skylease supervisor will review your application
  • Upload proof of renter's insurance and drivers license/State issued ID for further identification
  • Once uploaded, wait 1-5 business days for your application to reviewed. If there are errors, you will be emailed
