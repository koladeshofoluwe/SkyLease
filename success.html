Glad you've selected to check our surplus at SkyLease! This page offers in-depth photos and information about our aircrafts to rent!

Cessna 172

A popular single-engine aircraft great for training and scenic flights. Easy to fly and super reliable.

  • Top Speed: 140 knots
  • Range: 800 nautical miles
  • Engine: Lycoming IO-360
  • Seats: 4 (including pilot)
  • Avionics: Garmin G1000

Piper PA-28 Cherokee

Known for reliability and ease of handling, ideal for short range travel and training.

  • Top Speed: 125 knots
  • Range: 525 nautical miles
  • Engine: Lycoming O-320
  • Seats: 4 (including pilot)
  • Avionics: Garmin GNS 430 or G1000

Cirrus SR22

A high-performance single-engine aircraft known for its speed, luxury interior, and safety features.

  • Top Speed: 183 knots
  • Range: 1,200 nautical miles
  • Engine: Continental IO-550-N
  • Seats: 4 (with side yoke configuration)
  • Avionics: Garmin Perspective+ with synthetic vision
  • Notable Feature: CAPS (Cirrus Airframe Parachute System)

Cessna 208 Caravan

A versatile, high-capacity turboprop aircraft widely used for regional transport and cargo runs.

  • Top Speed: 186 knots
  • Range: 970 nautical miles
  • Engine: Pratt & Whitney PT6A turboprop
  • Seats: Up to 14
  • Avionics: Garmin G1000 NXi
  • Notable Feature: Charter, cargo, and remote-area travel
