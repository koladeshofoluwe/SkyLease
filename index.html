We Get Fly

SkyLease offers premium aircraft rentals for pilots, students, and aviation lovers. Whether you're flying solo or exploring the skies with friends, we've got you covered.

Explore Aircraft

Types of aircrafts offered:

Cessna 172L: A popular single-engine, four-seat aircraft great for student pilots and scenic flights.

Piper PA-28: Known for reliability and ease of handling, ideal for short-range travel and training.

Cirrus SR22: A high-performance aircraft with luxury interiors and advanced avionics systems.

Cessna 208 Caravan: A rugged, high-capacity aircraft built for group travel and regional hops.

Requirements to Rent

  • Must hold a valid pilot’s license (PPL or higher).

  • Logbook with at least 50 flight hours.

  • Completed checkout flight with SkyLease instructor.

  • Proof of renter’s insurance or purchase our rental coverage.
